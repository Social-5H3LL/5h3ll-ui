## Privacy Policy

Social5h3ll builds open-source UI tools and AI integrations. This page explains how we handle any data collected through 5H3LL-UI and its related sites and services.

### What We Collect

We do not collect personal data. 5H3LL-UI is a client-side component library — the CSS, JavaScript, and HTML templates are delivered to your browser directly from our CDN or via npm. No data about your usage is transmitted back to our servers.

### Analytics

We do not use third-party analytics, tracking pixels, or advertising networks on our properties. Server logs may include basic connection data such as IP address and user-agent for security and troubleshooting purposes only.

### Cookies

We do not use browser cookies. No session tokens, tracking identifiers, or preference data is stored in your browser.

### Third-Party Links

Our sites may link to external services (such as GitHub, npm, Cloudflare, or social media platforms). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those third parties.

### Contact

For privacy concerns or data questions, reach out via [GitHub](https://github.com/social-5h3ll/5h3ll-ui) or our social channels.
